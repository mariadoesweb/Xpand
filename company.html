Core values

This is also why we’ve spent time and effort defining our Core Values. We’re proud to share them - if they resonate with you, check out our open roles.

#Glass Doors

Trusted relationships are built with integrity and honesty in our interactions with colleagues and clients. Being transparent and straightforward is a way of life at Xpand.

#Drive Learning

We learn more when we take risks, fail without fear, and focus on the lessons. We strive to adopt a growth mindset as a team whilst designing best-in-class solutions for our clients.

#People First

We treat each other with respect at all times. We encourage open dialogue, constructive conversations, and value diverse opinions. We hold space, support each other, and lead in an inclusive.

#Succeed Together

Teamwork at Xpand translates to leveraging each one's unique skills and strengths. We synergize and achieve impactful outcomes collectively.

#Bold Ideas

Everyone at Xpand has the opportunity to paint the canvas and innovate. Bold ideation, intuitive insight, and out of the box thinking are the cornerstones of our vibrant workspace.

#Stay Agile

We take pride at being self-driven to take the lead, responsible for outcomes, and adaptable to change. We know that we hold much power in our own hands to succeed.

#Prioritize Life

Work revolves around life, not vice versa. We are mindful of personal priorities, individual space, and flexibility to enrich life holistically. We are great at what we do because of our life at Xpand and outside of it.
